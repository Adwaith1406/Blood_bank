Project Abstract

The Blood Bank Management System is a database-driven application designed to efficiently manage blood donor records, inventory, and hospital requests[cite: 3]. Developed by Group 2 using Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL to ensure accurate tracking and healthcare safety[cite: 6, 9]. This project aims to eliminate manual record keeping and contribute to patient well-being[cite: 4, 8].
